What makes it different

Ambient sensing

A light sensor reads your room's natural light every 90 seconds. AURA adjusts its warmth and brightness to complement — not compete with — what's already there.

Learns your rhythm

After two weeks, AURA understands your morning light, your evening wind-down, and the focused afternoon stretch. It transitions without being asked.

No-app setup

Plug in. Done. There is no app, no Wi-Fi pairing ritual, no firmware update on arrival. The single touch-sensitive base handles everything you'd ever want to change.
